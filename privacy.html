Calmi – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Calmi (“the App”) helps users cultivate emotional awareness through daily tracking, reflection, and mindful practices. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, store, and use.

Calmi is a fully offline, device-only application. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Calmi does NOT collect or send any of the following:

  • Personal information
  • Contacts
  • Health or medical data
  • Location data
  • Photos or media
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics or crash reports
  • Tracking or profiling data

The App does not communicate with external servers or third-party services.

AI Processing Transparency

Calmi does not transmit journal entries, emotion data, or any user content to external servers for AI processing. All features, including optional reflections, operate entirely on the device.

3. Data Stored on Your Device (Local-Only)

All data used by Calmi is stored only on your device. Nothing is uploaded, synced, or shared.

3.1 Emotion Control Records

  • Daily emotional control successes
  • Achievement progress

Stored locally and deleted only when you manually clear data.

3.2 Journal Entries (Joy & Confession)

Your journal text is stored strictly on your device. It is never uploaded or transmitted. You may delete individual entries or erase all data at any time.

3.3 Reminders

Notification preferences are stored locally and used only to schedule reminders. Local notifications are handled entirely on your device.

4. PIN Code & App Lock Security

If App Lock is enabled:

  • Your 4-digit PIN is securely stored in the iOS Keychain
  • PINs are never uploaded or shared
  • Biometric authentication is handled only by the operating system (Face ID / Touch ID)

Forgot your PIN?

Resetting App Lock requires clearing all app data from:
Settings → Clear All Data

5. How to Delete Your Data

You can delete all local data at any time:

Settings → Clear All Data

This permanently deletes:

  • Emotion records
  • Achievements
  • Joy & Confession entries
  • Reminder settings
  • App Lock PIN
  • All app preferences

6. Third-Party Services

Calmi uses no third-party SDKs, analytics, servers, or advertising services.

7. Children’s Privacy

Calmi does not collect personal data and does not require account creation. It is not intended as a medical or therapeutic tool.

8. Changes to This Policy

Any updates will change the “Last updated” date above. Calmi is offline, so changes apply only when you install a new version.
