Calmi – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Calmi (“the App”) helps users cultivate emotional awareness through daily tracking, reflection, and mindful practices. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, store, and use.

Calmi is a fully offline, device-only application. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Calmi does NOT collect or send any of the following:

  • Personal information
  • Contacts
  • Health or medical data
  • Location data
  • Photos or media
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics or crash reports
  • Tracking or profiling data

The App does not communicate with external servers or third-party services.

3. Data Stored on Your Device (Local-Only)

The App stores all user data only on your device. No data is uploaded, shared, or synced.

3.1 Emotion Control Records

  • Daily emotional control successes
  • Achievement progress

Stored locally and deleted only when you choose to clear data.

3.2 Journal Entries (Joy & Confession)

Your text entries remain strictly on your device. They are never transmitted. You may delete individual entries or use “Clear All Data”.

3.3 Reminders

Notification preferences are stored locally and used only for scheduling local notifications.

4. PIN Code & App Lock Security

If App Lock is enabled:

  • Your 4-digit PIN is stored securely in the iOS Keychain
  • The PIN is never uploaded
  • Biometrics (Face ID / Touch ID) are handled entirely by the system

Forgot your PIN?
Resetting App Lock requires clearing all app data from:
Settings → Data → Clear All Data

This clears Emotion data, Journals, Reminders, Achievements, and the PIN itself.

5. How to Delete Your Data

You can delete all local data at any time via:

Settings → Clear All Data

Calmi does not store backups or external copies.

6. Third-Party Services

Calmi uses no third-party SDKs, no analytics, no ads, and performs no network communication.

7. Children’s Privacy

Calmi does not collect personal data and does not require an account. It is not intended as a medical or therapeutic tool.

8. Changes to This Policy

Updates to this policy will modify the “Last updated” date. Since the App is offline, changes apply only to new versions you install.
